Frequently Asked
Questions

What do I stand to gain as a fresh Engineering Graduate?

You will agree with us that the skill gap between the engineering industry and the academic sector is very wide. Gone are those days where firms recruit fresh graduates with a view of training them to acquire the needed skills such as what we offer. What you stand to gain is knowledge and understanding that can help position you to getting employed in the oil and gas sector as a design engineer and once in, your growth process begins.

What discipline of Engineering can take this course?

The courses are designed for easy learning by anyone from any discipline (not restricted to engineering alone) with interest in the Oil and Gas sector.

Are these courses also offered as on-site trainings?

Very much so. These courses are offered as in-house training in our training facility in Benin, Nigeria. Information concerning the commencement of the training is always made  available when we are ready to host this and other training.
