ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are a team of experienced Professionals, trainers and facilitators, who have garnered much experience working as engineering consultants and technical services provider to top firms in the industry.

Together, we boast of a cumulative experience of   over 50 years, and we believe these experiences can be bequeathed.

We are passionate about learning and we continually seek better ways to meet the demands of our ever growing community of experienced professionals and active learners.

VISION

To be the best one-stop-shop for affordable, top-notch, online skilled based, professional development courses.

MISION

To create, develop, and deliver industry focused, skill-based professional development curricula courses for everyone willing to enhance their professional skills and career prospects through the easiest-to-use online learning platform.

Why Choose Us?

At the top of our unique position is our ability to integrate the industrial working culture into our curriculum. 
Our students enjoy the benefit of partaking in real-life industrial projects as part of their classroom experience.
We are able to achieve this because, unlike most training academies, we are first an Engineering Company before a training Academy.
